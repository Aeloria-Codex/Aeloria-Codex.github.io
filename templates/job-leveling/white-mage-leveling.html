General Tips for White Mage

White Mage is a powerful healer known for its ability to heal, shield, and provide offensive support.

Early levels focus on healing and using Cure spells.

Keep an eye on your MP and use your spells strategically.

Learn to weave in your damage spells, like Stone and Aero, between healing spells. This will help you build up your "Cure" gauge for stronger heals.

Unlocking "Cure II" at level 15 is a game-changer. It lets you heal your party members for a greater amount of HP.

At level 30, you gain access to the powerful "Holy" ability, which is a powerful AoE healing spell that's great for healing multiple party members.

Learn the importance of managing your MP and using it efficiently to maximize your healing output.

Your job quests will provide valuable new abilities, including the critical "Medica II," which allows you to heal your entire party for a large amount of HP.

Always be aware of your party members' HP and use your spells to keep them topped off.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Levels 1-14: Use Cure/Physick, and weave in Stone/Aero when available.

  • Levels 15-29: Use Cure II/Physick II, and weave in Stone/Aero when available.

  • Levels 30-39: Use Cure II/Physick II, Holy, and weave in Stone/Aero when available.

  • Levels 40-49: Use Cure II/Physick II, Holy, Medica, and weave in Stone/Aero when available.

  • Levels 50-59: Use Cure II/Physick II, Holy, Medica, and weave in Stone/Aero when available.

  • Levels 60-69: Use Cure II/Physick II, Holy, Medica, and weave in Stone/Aero when available.
