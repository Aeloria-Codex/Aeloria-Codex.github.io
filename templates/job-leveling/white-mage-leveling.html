General Tips for White Mage

  • Keep your party alive by prioritizing healing with abilities like Tetragrammaton and Medica III. Positioning is key to maximize AoE healing coverage.

  • Manage your MP efficiently using Thin Air and Assize. Keep an eye on your cooldowns to maintain sustained healing output.

  • Utilize your damage spells like Dia and Glare III when healing isnâ€™t immediately required to contribute to the party's overall damage output.

  • Time your cooldowns like Temperance and Plenary Indulgence during heavy damage phases to mitigate damage and provide additional healing boosts.

  • Activate Temperance during raid-wide damage phases to boost healing and reduce party damage.

  • Use Liturgy of the Bell in advance of unavoidable damage to maximize its healing potential over its duration.

  • Use Thin Air strategically when casting MP-intensive spells like Medica III or Holy III.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Use Glare III as your main damage-dealing spell against single targets.

  • Apply Dia to maintain DoT uptime on the enemy, refreshing it as it expires.

  • Heal your tank or other party members with Cure II or Tetragrammaton for burst healing.

  • Use Divine Benison preemptively to absorb incoming damage on your target.

Multi Target

  • Cast Holy III to deal AoE damage and stun enemies for 4 seconds.

  • Use Assize for AoE damage and party-wide healing, especially during high-damage situations.

  • Heal the party with Medica III for sustained healing over time in multi-target fights.

  • If multiple party members are taking heavy damage, use Afflatus Rapture to provide an immediate AoE heal and charge your Blood Lily.
