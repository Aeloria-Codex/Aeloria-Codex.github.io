General Tips for Dragoon

Positionals: Many of your abilities deal more damage from certain positions. Practice hitting these consistently to maximize your damage output.

Buff Management: Coordinate your Battle Litany with your party's burst windows for maximum effectiveness.

Life of the Dragon: Always aim to use all three Nastrond and one Stardiver during each Life of the Dragon window.

Mobility: Use Elusive Jump and Spineshatter Dive for movement without sacrificing damage. Be cautious with Elusive Jump's backwards motion.

AOE Efficiency: In dungeons, save Dragonfire Dive for trash packs when possible, as it's one of your strongest AOE abilities.

Rotation Overview

The Dragoon's rotation revolves around maintaining your combos, managing your jumps, and maximizing your damage during Life of the Dragon phases. The focus is on single-target and multi-target abilities, with special emphasis on positional attacks and buff alignment.

Single Target Rotation

  • Main Combo: Raiden Thrust → Disembowel → Chaotic Spring → Wheeling Thrust → Fang and Claw

  • Alternate Combo: Raiden Thrust → Vorpal Thrust → Heavens' Thrust → Fang and Claw → Wheeling Thrust

  • Use Life Surge on Heavens' Thrust or Fang and Claw, prioritizing buff windows.

  • Activate Lance Charge and Battle Litany on cooldown after the opener.

  • Use High Jump, Spineshatter Dive, and Dragonfire Dive on cooldown.

  • Always use Mirage Dive after jumps before its 15-second timer expires.

  • Use Geirskogul on cooldown, which turns into Nastrond during Life of the Dragon.

  • Use Stardiver once during each Life of the Dragon window, preferably under buffs.

Multi-Target Rotation

  • For 3+ targets, use Doomspike → Sonic Thrust → Coerthan Torment combo.

  • Weave in Dragonfire Dive and Geirskogul for additional AOE damage.

  • Use Nastrond and Stardiver during Life of the Dragon for powerful AOE damage.

  • Continue using your buffs (Lance Charge, Battle Litany) on cooldown.

  • Use Wyrmwind Thrust when available for additional AOE damage.

Mobility and Utility

  • Use Elusive Jump to quickly move away from danger or return to the boss after mechanics.

  • Spineshatter Dive and Dragonfire Dive can be used as gap closers when needed.

  • Piercing Talon can be used for ranged attacks when unable to reach the boss.

  • Use Feint to reduce physical damage from boss attacks.

  • Second Wind provides a small self-heal when needed.
