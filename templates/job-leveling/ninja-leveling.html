Job Tips

  • Kuani Gauge unlocks at level 45. Use Armor Crush to gain Kuani and Aeolian Edge to spend them.

  • Ninki Gauge unlocks at level 62. Utilize abilities during Mug, Dokumori, Trick Attack, and Kunai's Bane buffs, try to avvoid exceeding the gauge limit.

  • Use Hellfrog Medium when there is 3 more enemies.

  • Cast Meisui on cooldown.

  • Your best way to gain Kunai is to use Armor Crush. Each use will give you 2 stacks up to 5.

Single Target Priority

  • Start with Suiton so you will enable the use of Kuani's Bane

  • Next use Ration and make sure to spend the reamining charges during buffs

  • If Kassatsu is avaliale priorities using Hyosho Ranryu

  • Execute Ten Chi Jin: Follow the sequence of Ten > Chi > Jin to perform Fuma Shuriken > Raiton > Suiton. This will form your standard single-target rotation.

  • Generate Kunai: Use Spinning Edge > Gust Slash > Armor Crush to build Kunai.

  • Damage Combo: If you have Kunai available, execute Spinning Edge > Gust Slash > Aeolian Edge.

  • Use Phantom Kamaitachi: Activate this within your buffs whenever possible, or when you need to reposition away from your target.

Multi-Target Priority

Combo Sequence: For encounters with 3 or more targets, start with Death Blossom followed by Hakke Mujinsatsu as your main combo.

  • Katon:Use Katon while ensuring Doton is maintained over Raiton when facing 3 or more enemies.

  • Goka Mekkyaku:Prioritize using Goka Mekkyaku over Hyosho Ranryu in multi-target situations.

  • Set Up for Kunai's Bane:Utilize Huton to prepare for Kunai's Bane.
