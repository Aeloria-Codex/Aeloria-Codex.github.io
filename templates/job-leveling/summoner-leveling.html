Rotation Overview

Summoners wield primal power to decimate foes and support allies. Keep these strategies in mind:

  • Summon Carbuncle: Available at level 10, Carbuncle assists with damage and utility.

  • Aetherflow Stacks: Use these wisely for Fester and Energy Drain to enhance your damage and sustain MP.

  • Ruin II: Utilize this instant-cast spell to weave abilities and reposition effectively.

  • Bahamut and Phoenix: Unlocked at levels 30 and 80, respectively, these summons drastically increase your damage potential.

Leveling Rotations

Single Target Rotation

  • Levels 1-29: Spam Ruin . Summon Carbuncle and use its abilities.

  • Levels 30-49: Use Ruin II , Fester (with Aetherflow, and Summon Bahamut abilities.

  • Levels 50-79: Cast Ruin III , Fester , and Summon Bahamut . Use Swiftcast to maximize mobility.

  • Levels 80-90: Incorporate Phoenix Summon . Use Ruin IV when available.

Multi-Target Rotation

  • Levels 1-29: Spam Ruin . Use Carbuncle abilities.

  • Levels 30-49: Use Fester and Carbuncle AOE abilities when available.

  • Levels 50-79: Use Tri-Disaster for DOTs and AOE damage. Summon Bahamut to finish enemies.

  • Levels 80-90: Summon Phoenix and use its AOE abilities to burn through large packs.
