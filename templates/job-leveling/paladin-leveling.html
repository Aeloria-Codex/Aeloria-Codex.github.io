General Tips for Paladin

Tank Stance: Always ensure Iron Will is activated when tanking in dungeons or holding aggro in raids.

Getting Agro: Make sure that you are running up to the mobs and using your AOE abilities to make sure you are securing agro.

Mob Positioning: Make sure you are positioning all the mobs as tight as possible to make sure your dps and healers can using their AOE's effectively.

Offensive Cooldowns: Make sure you are not burning all or too many of your Mits and that your are spreading them out correctly.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Level 15-25: Alternate Fast Blade and Riot Blade. Use Fight or Flight late in the GCD after Fast Blade.

  • Level 26-59: Use the combo: Fast Blade → Riot Blade → Rage of Halone

  • Level 54+: Add Goring Blade during Fight or Flight (doesn't break combo)

  • Level 60+: Rage of Halone upgrades to Royal Authority

  • Level 64+: Royal Authority grants Divine Might for empowered Holy Spirit

  • Level 68+: Use Requiescat inside Fight or Flight, followed by 4x Holy Spirit

Multi Target

  • Level 15-39: Spam Total Eclipse

  • Level 40+: Use the combo: Total Eclipse → Prominence

  • Level 72+: Prominence combo grants Divine Might

  • Level 72+: Use Holy Circle when you have Requiescat stacks or Divine Might

  • Level 80+: Replace first Holy Circle with Confiteor
