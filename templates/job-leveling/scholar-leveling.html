General Tips for Scholar

Maintain Aetherflow stacks to power your healing toolkit.

Utilize Adloquium and Sacred Soil to provide shielding and damage mitigation.

Weave in oGCD abilities like Excogitation, Dissipation, and Deployment Tactics to supplement your GCD heals.

Take advantage of your pet, Eos or Selene, to provide passive healing and utility.

Prioritize Broil and Art of War to contribute meaningful damage during fights.

Use Emergency Tactics to convert your shielding into direct healing when needed.

Become familiar with Recitation and Expedient to enable powerful heal bursts.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Levels 1-29: Cast Physick for healing, use Ruin for damage, summon and maintain your pet.

  • Levels 30-44: Incorporate Adloquium for shielding, weave in Excogitation and Energy Drain .

  • Levels 45-59: Add Biolysis DoT and use Lustrate for burst healing.

  • Levels 60-69: Utilize Sacred Soil for damage mitigation, pair Deployment Tactics with Adloquium.

  • Levels 70-79: Incorporate Recitation for instant-cast Adloquium or Excogitation. Use Emergency Tactics to convert shielding into direct healing.

  • Levels 80-90: Maximize the synergy between Expedient and Adloquium/Excogitation for powerful heal bursts. Prioritize Broil III for damage output.

Multi-Target

  • Levels 1-29: Use Succor for AoE shielding, supplement with Eos or Selene pet heals.

  • Levels 30-59: Maintain Art of War DoT, use Indomitability and Sacred Soil for AoE healing and mitigation.

  • Levels 60-69: Leverage Chain Strategem to amplify your party's DPS during trash pulls.

  • Levels 70-79: Utilize Dissipation to empower your healing toolkit during AoE-heavy scenarios.
