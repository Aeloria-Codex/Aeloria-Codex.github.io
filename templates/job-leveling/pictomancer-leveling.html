General Tips for Pictomancer

Resource Management: Keep a close eye on Ink reserves to ensure you’re ready to unleash powerful abilities. Use your basic spells to build ink without overspending on minor attacks.

AoE Setup: Position yourself strategically to maximize the reach of AoE spells. Group enemies together when possible, allowing you to hit multiple targets and conserve Ink for high-priority skills.

Cooldown Timing: Don’t stack cooldowns unnecessarily. Plan your spells to keep Ink-efficient spells active and reserve major cooldowns for peak damage moments.

Positioning and Mobility: Use your movement abilities to maintain an ideal distance from bosses and avoid heavy damage zones while staying within casting range for your AoE.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Sub-Level 30: Use Fire (Red) → Water (Blue) → Aero (Green) . Employ Smudge for movement and Tempera Coat for damage reduction.

  • Level 30-50: Leverage Creature Muses strategically, prioritizing AoE damage while preventing overcapping.

  • Level 50-60: Implement Hammer Motif on cooldown, utilizing the 30-second window for optimal positioning.

  • Level 60-70: Incorporate longer cast spells like Blizzard (Cyan) , Stone (Yellow) , and Thunder (Magenta) during stationary phases.

  • Level 70-80: Maximize Starry Muse effectiveness by using in opener, on cooldown, and strategically within buff windows.

  • Level 80-90: Integrate Holy (White) for enhanced movement and supplemental damage during Starry Muse .

  • Level 90-92: Execute Comet (Black) following Subtractive Palette .

  • Level 92-96: Utilize pre-pull Rainbow Drip and Swiftcast technique before encounter downtime.

  • Level 96-100: Unlock and optimize Madeen , prioritizing its use within Starry Muse for superior potency.

Multi Target

    • Sub-Level 30: Adapt spell sequence based on target count, transitioning from single-target to multi-target strategies.

    • Level 30-50: Emphasize Creature Muses and Moogle for AoE damage, preparing during inter-pull intervals.

    • Level 50-54: Leverage Hammer charges for widespread critical damage across multiple targets.

    • Level 54-60: Refine spell combinations according to target density, introducing enhanced AoE variants.

    • Level 60-70: Maintain consistent AoE priorities while introducing Subtractive Palette nuances.

    • Level 70-80: Incorporate raid buffs strategically during dungeon encounters.

    • Level 80-90: Prioritize Holy for superior multi-target damage potential.

    • Level 90-94: Integrate Comet after Muse applications when Subtractive Palette is available.

    • Level 94-100: Optimize spell selection for four or more targets, balancing Comet , Holy , and Subtractive Palette techniques.

    • Level 100: Implement Star Prism post-raid buff, following established AoE priority frameworks.
