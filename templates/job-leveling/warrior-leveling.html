General Tips for Warrior

Warrior is a powerful melee DPS known for its high damage output and ability to tank in a pinch. It's a great choice for players who like to be in the thick of the action.

Early levels focus on building up your "Inner Beast" gauge and using your basic attacks to deal damage.

Unlocking the "Inner Release" ability at level 15 is a game-changer. It lets you unleash a burst of damage and activate your "Inner Release" mode, increasing your attack power.

Learn to weave in your combo skills between basic attacks, focusing on building up "Inner Beast" as fast as possible.

At level 30, you gain access to the powerful "Fell Cleave" ability, which is a powerful AoE attack that's great for dealing with multiple enemies.

Learn the importance of managing your "Inner Beast" gauge and using it to maximize your damage output.

Your job quests will provide valuable new abilities, including the critical "Stormblood" ability, which allows you to unleash a devastating attack and increase your attack power.

Keep an eye on your "Blood Gauge" and use it to heal yourself when necessary.

Don't be afraid to use your "Overpower" ability to deal extra damage when you need to.
