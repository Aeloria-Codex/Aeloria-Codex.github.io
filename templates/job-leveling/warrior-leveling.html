General Tips for Warrior

  • Always maintain Surging Tempest for a consistent 10% damage increase. Use Storm's Eye or Mythril Tempest to refresh it.

  • Use Berserk and Inner Release strategically to maximize burst damage, prioritizing Fell Cleave and Decimate during their windows.

  • Keep your Beast Gauge above 50 to ensure you can use powerful abilities like Fell Cleave, Decimate, or Inner Chaos at crucial moments.

  • Plan your defensive cooldowns like Vengeance, Holmgang, and Raw Intuition to mitigate incoming damage effectively during boss mechanics.

  • Make good use of healing abilities such as Equilibrium and Bloodwhetting to reduce pressure on healers.

  • Holmgang is a lifesaving cooldown—use it during moments of unavoidable lethal damage.

  • For large pulls, use Vengeance and Shake It Off to reduce damage taken and shield your team.

  • Don’t forget to utilize Onslaught for gap-closing or to quickly engage enemies.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Open with Heavy Swing, followed by Maim, and then Storm's Eye to establish Surging Tempest.

  • Continue with Heavy Swing → Maim → Storm's Path for additional damage and HP restoration.

  • Use Infuriate to generate 50 Beast Gauge and enable abilities like Inner Chaos.

  • During Inner Release, prioritize using Fell Cleave for massive critical hits.

  • Use Upheaval and Primal Rend when available for significant burst damage.

Multi Target

  • Start with Overpower to establish enmity on multiple targets.

  • Use Mythril Tempest to apply Surging Tempest for a 10% damage buff.

  • Spam Decimate when Beast Gauge is available to deal consistent AoE damage.

  • During Inner Release, prioritize Decimate for free critical hits on all enemies.

  • Include Chaotic Cyclone if under the effect of Nascent Chaos.
