General Tips for Machinist

Resource Management: Keep an eye on your Heat and Battery gauges to ensure you don't waste resources. Use Hypercharge before the Heat Gauge maxes out, and summon Automaton Queen before the Battery Gauge fills completely.

Reassemble: Always pair Reassemble with your strongest available attack, such as Drill or Air Anchor, to maximize damage output.

Cooldown Timing: Align offensive cooldowns like Wildfire with powerful abilities to deal significant damage during bursts. Pairing them with group raid buffs can further enhance your performance.

Mitigation: Utilize the Machinist's mitigation tools like Dismantle and Tactician to support your team and reduce incoming damage in both dungeon and raid settings.

Rotation Overview

The Machinist's rotation focuses on managing your resources, ensuring key abilities are used on cooldown, and dealing consistent damage. Below is an overview of single-target and multi-target rotations to guide you through different encounters.

Single Target Priority

  • Use Drill every 20 seconds.

  • Use Hot Shot every 40 seconds.

  • Use Air Anchor every 40 seconds.

  • Use Chain Saw every 60 seconds.

  • Activate Hypercharge to use Blazing Shot x5 when above 50 heat or when free usage is available via Barrel Stabilizer. Pair with Wildfire every 120 seconds.

  • Fill with Heated Split Shot → Heated Slug Shot → Clean Shot combo while waiting for cooldowns.

Multi-Target Priority

  • For three or more targets, use Hypercharge to trigger Blazing Shot or Auto Crossbow, depending on the situation.

  • Use Bioblaster on two or more targets.

  • Employ Chain Saw and Excavator for additional AoE damage.

Mitigation and Utility

  • Use Dismantle to reduce enemy damage output during key moments.

  • Activate Tactician to reduce AoE damage taken by your party.

  • Deploy Reassemble with your highest damage ability for optimal burst.
