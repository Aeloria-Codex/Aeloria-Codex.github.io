General Tips for Bard

Starting at level 1: Keep in mind that Bard begins at level 1.

Job Quests: Always complete your job quests! They unlock essential skills and abilities as you level up.

DoT Uptime: As a Bard you have a lot of Dot (Damage over Time) skills, make sure to keep your DoT up to continue doing damage.

Peloton: Make sure to keep up Peloton for your party when out of combat.

Role Actions: Besides Peloton you will have other Role Actions such as Second Wind and Interupts or Status Effect abilities. Make sure you are using them properly.

Job Gauge: Keep an eye on your Job Gauge so you do not overcap on charges.

Rotation Overview

Bard's core rotation remains consistent as you level, with added abilities and song cycles enhancing your kit. Here is a breakdown of the essential rotations you should focus on:

Single Target Openers

  • Stormbite → The Wanderer’s Minuet → Empyreal Arrow

  • Caustic Bite → wait → Battle Voice

  • Burst Shot or Refulgent Arrow → Radiant Finale

Song Cycle

Bard’s rotation revolves around its song cycle. You will alternate between The Wanderer’s Minuet, Mage’s Ballad, and Army’s Paeon, each with specific buffs to manage:

  • The Wanderer’s Minuet: Provides a damage buff and grants access to Refulgent Arrow.

  • Mage’s Ballad: Reduces cooldowns on Empyreal Arrow and grants additional proc chances.

  • Army’s Paeon: Increases attack speed and transitions into a speed buff post-use.

Single Target Rotation

Once your opener is complete, focus on maintaining your DoTs (Stormbite, Caustic Bite) and firing Empyreal Arrow when available. Your rotation should prioritize:

  • Maintain DoTs on your target.

  • Use Empyreal Arrow on cooldown.

  • Fire Refulgent Arrow when it procs.

  • Weave Barrage with Refulgent Arrow for maximum damage.

Burst Sequence

During burst windows (such as party buffs), maximize your damage by stacking cooldowns:

  • Use Radiant Finale after building gauge from songs.

  • Weave Battle Voice for party-wide damage boosts.

AoE Rotation

For multiple targets (3+), focus on applying your AoE DoTs and using area-of-effect abilities:

  • Apply Rain of Death and Quick Nock to maximize AoE damage.

  • Weave Empyreal Arrow and other cooldowns into your AoE rotation.
