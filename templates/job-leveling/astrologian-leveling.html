General Tips for Astrologian

  • Starting at level 30: Keep in mind that Astrologian begins at level 30, but your level may and will be synced down in lower-level content.
  • Job Quests: Always complete your job quests! They unlock essential skills and abilities as you level up.
  • Keep casting: Even as a healer, you should always aim to deal damage when healing isn’t required.
  • Lucid Dreaming: Manage your MP by using Lucid Dreaming at around 80-85% MP.
  • Lightspeed: Use Lightspeed to maintain uptime while moving or to cast faster in tight situations.
  • Esuna: Keep an eye on debuffs and use Esuna to remove them when necessary.
  • Swiftcast and Revives: Use Swiftcast to revive allies, but if it’s on cooldown, don’t hesitate to hard-cast Ascend.

Single-Target Healing

Use The Spire , The Bole , and The Ewer on the tank when they’re taking damage.

The Arrow can boost healing on the tank or another target, especially if timed with regens or shields.

Use Exaltation on the tank during pulls to reduce damage, but avoid overlapping it with their strongest cooldowns or invulnerability.

Essential Dignity is your go-to single-target heal. Use it freely rather than saving it for emergencies.

With three charges, Essential Dignity allows more flexibility for emergencies while keeping the other charges in use.

Celestial Intersection is best for tank upkeep thanks to its shield and short cooldown. Use it whenever possible.


AoE Healing

Earthly Star primarily serves as a damage source in dungeons but also provides AoE healing when detonated strategically.

Collective Unconscious offers instant mitigation and healing, allowing you to keep casting while moving.

Celestial Opposition offers higher potency than Collective Unconscious. Use it when party-wide healing is needed.

Lady of Crowns provides instant healing, equaling Helios in potency but lacks the additional benefits offered by Celestial Opposition .

Horoscope delivers a 200-potency heal on a short cooldown. While modest, it effectively bridges healing gaps between other off-global cooldown abilities.

Macrocosmos functions as both a multi-target DPS increase and a potent AoE heal, capable of fully restoring a tank’s HP when under heavy damage.


Single-Target Damage

Combust III: Maintain DoT on your target whenever it will tick for at least 12 seconds.

Fall Malefic: Cast this regularly to maximize damage output.

Divination: Use during burst windows to amplify party damage.


AoE Damage

Combust III: Apply this DoT on multiple targets for prolonged engagements.

Gravity II: Use on 3 or more targets; otherwise, cast Fall Malefic.

Divination + Lord of Crowns: + Combine these for a potent AoE burst during large pulls.
