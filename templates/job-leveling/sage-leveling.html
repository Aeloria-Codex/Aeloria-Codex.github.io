General Tips for Sage

Maintain Kardia buff on the tank to provide consistent healing throughout dungeons.

Utilize Eukrasian Diagnosis and Eukrasian Prognosis to provide shielding in addition to healing.

Weave in oGCD abilities like Soteria, Physis, and Druochole to supplement your GCD heals.

Generate Addersgall stacks to empower your single-target and AoE healing tools.

Optimize Eukrasian Dosis uptime to contribute consistent damage in single-target fights.

Leverage Dyskrasia and Toxikon for effective AoE damage during trash pulls.

Become familiar with using Haima, Panhaima, and Kerachole to mitigate incoming damage.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Levels 18-29: Cast Dosis , use Phlegma charges, maintain Kardia on tank.

  • Levels 30-44: Apply Eukrasian Dosis DoT, use Eukrasian Diagnosis for healing, weave in oGCDs like Soteria .

  • Levels 45-65: Add Druochole and Ixochole for Addersgall-empowered healing, continue Eukrasian Dosis uptime.

  • Levels 66-80: Utilize Haima and Panhaima for powerful single-target and AoE shielding, maintain Eukrasian Dosis.

  • Levels 81-90: Incorporate Krasis to boost healing received, use Pneuma as a powerful healing GCD.

  • Levels 91-100: Maximize the synergy between Philosophia , Physis , and Eukrasian Prognosis II for enhanced party survivability.

Multi-Target

  • Levels 18-45: Use Dyskrasia for AoE damage, supplement with Physis and Ixochole heals.

  • Levels 46-65: Continue Dyskrasia spam, weave in Toxikon when you have Addersting stacks.

  • Levels 66-80: Maintain Eukrasian Dyskrasia DoT, use Panhaima and Holos for AoE mitigation and healing.

  • Levels 81-90: Leverage Pneuma for both damage and healing during trash pulls.

  • Levels 91-100: Synergize Eukrasian Prognosis II with Philosophia and Physis to provide robust party-wide shielding.
