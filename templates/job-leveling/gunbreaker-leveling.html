Getting Started

Welcome to the Gunbreaker guide! This resource is designed to assist you in mastering the Gunbreaker job in Final Fantasy XIV, providing core strategies, essential tips, and a detailed breakdown of your rotation.

Note: Gunbreaker is unlocked at level 60, so you'll have access to a significant portion of your kit right from the start. This guide covers strategies from level 60 onwards.

The goal is to help you grasp the mindset behind your job and to offer some useful approaches as you level and tackle endgame content.

Gear Recommendations

Keeping your equipment up-to-date is crucial. Insufficient gear can hinder your damage output and survivability. Aim to have gear that matches your current level or is only slightly lower.

While progressing through the Main Scenario Quest (MSQ), you will frequently acquire gear upgrades and coffers to keep your setup competitive.

For leveling outside the MSQ, gear can be obtained through vendors (using Gil), the Market Board, or by trading Tomestones and Poetics earned through roulettes and other activities.

Gear Recommendations by Key Levels:

  • Level 60: Augmented Shire Gear (IL 270) – Available from Hismena in Idyllshire.

  • Level 70: Augmented Scaevan Gear (IL 400) – Obtainable from Enna in Rhalgr's Reach.

  • Level 80: Augmented Cryptlurker Gear (IL 530) – Purchase from Aymark in Eulmore.

  • Level 90: Augmented Credendum Gear (IL 660) – Obtainable from Cihanti in Radz-at-Han.

  • Level 100: Neo Kingdom Gear (IL 700) – Available from Zircon in Solution Nine.

General Tips for Gunbreaker

Tank Stance: Always activate Royal Guard when main tanking to maintain aggro.

Cartridge Management: Avoid overcapping on cartridges. Use them efficiently with Gnashing Fang combo and Burst Strike.

No Mercy Windows: Align your strongest abilities with No Mercy for maximum damage output.

Defensive Cooldowns: Rotate through your defensive abilities to maintain consistent damage mitigation.

Aurora Usage: Use Aurora proactively to mitigate incoming damage or to recover health over time.

Rotation Overview

The Gunbreaker's rotation revolves around managing your cartridge gauge, aligning your strongest abilities with No Mercy, and balancing damage output with defensive cooldowns. The focus is on single-target and multi-target abilities, with special emphasis on the Gnashing Fang combo and cartridge management.

Single Target Rotation

  • Activate Royal Guard when tanking.

  • Use No Mercy in your opener and every 60 seconds thereafter.

  • Main combo: Keen Edge → Brutal Shell → Solid Barrel

  • Spend two cartridges on Double Down every minute.

  • Use Gnashing Fang combo and Burst Strike for remaining cartridges.

  • Use Sonic Break and Bow Shock during every No Mercy window.

  • Weave Continuation after each part of the Gnashing Fang combo and after Burst Strike.

  • Use Danger Zone (upgrades to Blasting Zone at level 80) on cooldown.

  • Use Bloodfest on cooldown, ensuring you've spent cartridges first.

Multi-Target Rotation

  • For 3+ targets, use Demon Slice → Demon Slaughter combo.

  • Spend cartridges on Fated Circle for 4+ targets.

  • Use No Mercy, Bow Shock, and Sonic Break on cooldown.

  • Weave Continuation after Fated Circle.

  • Use Bloodfest on cooldown to generate cartridges.

Defensive Cooldowns and Utility

  • Use Heart of Corundum frequently for active mitigation.

  • Rampart and Nebula (upgrades to Great Nebula) for heavy incoming damage.

  • Superbolide for emergency invulnerability (reduces HP to 1).

  • Aurora for healing over time (has two charges).

  • Heart of Light to mitigate party-wide magic damage.

  • Reprisal to reduce damage from nearby enemies.

  • Arm's Length to prevent knockbacks or slow enemies.

  • Low Blow to stun and Interject to interrupt enemy abilities.
