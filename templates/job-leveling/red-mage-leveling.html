General Tips for Red Mage

Balance Black and White mana while casting spells to maintain effectiveness.

Utilize Dualcast procs efficiently to maximize damage output.

Learn to weave oGCD abilities between spells for optimal performance.

Make effective use of mobility tools like Corps-a-corps and Engagement .

Master the melee combo system when sufficient mana is accumulated.

Use Acceleration strategically to guarantee important procs.

Keep Embolden aligned with burst windows to maximize party damage.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Levels 18-25: Jolt into Verthunder /Veraero alternating, use Enchanted Riposte at 20|20 mana.

  • Levels 26-35: Use Verfire /Verstone procs when available, otherwise Jolt into Verthunder /Veraero , Enchanted Riposte at 20|20.

  • Levels 35-49: Add Enchanted Zwerchhau to your melee combo at 35|35 mana, continue magic rotation.

  • Level 50: Complete melee combo with Enchanted Redoublement at 50|50 mana, use Acceleration for guaranteed procs.

  • Levels 60-70: Add Verholy /Verflare finishers after melee combos, utilize Manafication for additional combos.

  • Levels 71-90: Incorporate Scorch and Resolution into combo finishers, maintain buff alignment with Embolden .

Multi Target (3+ enemies)

  • Levels 18-49: Verthunder II /Veraero II into Scatter , alternate between spells.

  • Levels 50-60: Add Acceleration usage on Scatter , begin incorporating oGCD damage abilities.

  • Levels 61-70: Use Impact instead of Scatter , maintain balanced AoE rotation.

  • Levels 71-90: Use Enchanted Moulinet combo at 50|50 mana, follow with Verholy /Verflare > Scorch > Resolution .
