Tips

Viper is a unique job with a strong focus on positioning and maximizing damage. This is achieved through its various stance-based abilities and venom effects.

  • Prioritize flanking and rear attacks for significant potency bonuses with abilities like Flanksbane Fang and Hindsbane Fang.

  • Utilize Slither to reposition strategically and capitalize on flank or rear positioning for higher damage output.

  • Keep track of your Venom effects and utilize them to increase your potency. Viper venom effects are applied by landing specific weaponskills or abilities, and they can stack for even greater damage.

  • Build your Serpent Offerings Gauge to unlock powerful attacks like Reawaken, which can be used to significantly increase your overall damage output.

Leveling Rotations

Single Target Rotation

The main goal of your single target rotation is to maximize your damage output by utilizing your venom effects and stance bonuses effectively.

  • Begin by landing Steel Fangs to gain Honed Reavers, which increases the potency of Reaving Fangs.

  • Follow with Reaving Fangs to gain Honed Steel, which increases the potency of Steel Fangs.

  • Continue cycling between Steel Fangs and Reaving Fangs, maintaining the buffs for maximum potency.

  • When available, utilize Serpent's Tail to unleash powerful attacks like Death Rattle, which can significantly boost your damage output.

  • Once you have enough Serpent Offerings Gauge, use Reawaken to unleash a burst of damage and gain more Anguine Tribute stacks for future attacks.

  • Use your Vicewinder charges to execute Hunter's Coil or Swiftskin's Coil, further boosting your potency and generating Serpent Offerings Gauge.

  • Continue to cycle between your high potency weaponskills and abilities, maintaining your buffs and utilizing your positioning to maximize damage output.

Multi-Target Rotation

When facing multiple targets, your goal is to distribute damage evenly across the group while still prioritizing your venom effects.

  • Use Steel Maw or Reaving Maw to apply initial damage and venom effects to multiple enemies.

  • Utilize your Slither ability to reposition and maximize your damage output by flanking or attacking from the rear.

  • Prioritize high potency abilities that deal damage to all enemies in the area, like Bloodied Maw or Jagged Maw, while maintaining your venom effects.

  • Utilize Uncoiled Fury to deal significant damage to the first enemy in the area, as well as a decent amount of damage to surrounding targets.

  • Build Serpent Offerings Gauge and use Reawaken to unleash a powerful burst of AoE damage.

  • Utilize your Vicewinder charges to execute Vicepit, dealing high AoE damage and generating Serpent Offerings Gauge.

  • Continue cycling between your high potency AoE weaponskills and abilities, maintaining your venom effects and maximizing damage output across the group.
