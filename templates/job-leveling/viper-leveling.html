General Tips for Viper

Viper is a powerful ranged physical DPS known for its quick, deadly strikes and potent poisons.

Early levels focus on building up your poison stacks and using your basic attacks to deal damage.

Keep an eye on your Venom gauge and use it strategically to apply poisons and unleash powerful combos.

Unlocking the "Serpent's Kiss" ability at level 15 is a game-changer. It lets you apply a potent poison that will drastically increase your damage output.

Learn to weave in your DoTs between basic attacks and combos. Your DoTs are a crucial source of your damage, especially later in the game.

At level 30, you gain access to the powerful "Serpent's Bite" ability. This ability gives you a stronger poison stack, significantly increasing your damage output.

Learn the importance of building up your venom gauge and managing your poison stacks to maximize your DPS.

Your job quests will provide valuable new abilities, including the critical "Viper's Bite," which allows you to unleash a devastating attack at the cost of some Venom.

Single Target

  • Levels 1-14: Use your basic attacks and Serpent's Kiss on cooldown.

  • Levels 15-29: Use Serpent's Kiss, Venomous Bite, and weave in your basic attacks.

  • Levels 30-39: Use Serpent's Bite, Venomous Bite, and weave in your basic attacks.
