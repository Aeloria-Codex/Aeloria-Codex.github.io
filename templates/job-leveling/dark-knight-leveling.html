General Tips for Dark Knight

Tank Stance: Always ensure Grit is activated when tanking in dungeons or holding aggro in raids.

Resource Management: Balance your usage of Mana and Blood. Use Edge of Shadow to maintain Darkside buff and Bloodspiller to avoid overcapping on Blood.

The Blackest Night: This is your most powerful defensive tool. Use it frequently, especially during trash pulls or to mitigate tankbusters.

Living Dead: Coordinate with your healers when using this invulnerability skill. Remember, you need to receive healing equal to your max HP within 10 seconds after it triggers.

Offensive Cooldowns: Align Delirium and Living Shadow with raid buffs when possible for maximum damage output.

Rotation Overview

The Dark Knight's rotation revolves around maintaining threat, managing resources, and maximizing damage output while staying alive. The focus is on single-target and multi-target abilities, with special emphasis on Mana and Blood management.

Single Target Rotation

  • Start with Grit activated to maintain aggro.

  • Use Hard Slash → Syphon Strike → Souleater as your main combo.

  • Weave in Edge of Shadow to maintain Darkside buff and deal extra damage.

  • Use Bloodspiller to spend Blood, prioritizing use during raid buffs.

  • Activate Delirium every 60 seconds, using Scarlet Delirium → Comeuppance → Torcleaver .

  • Use Salted Earth followed by Salt and Darkness every 90 seconds.

  • Employ Carve and Spit every 60 seconds for additional damage and resources.

  • Summon your Living Shadow every 120 seconds for sustained damage.

Multi-Target Rotation

  • For 3+ targets, use Unleash → Stalwart Soul as your main combo.

  • Use Flood of Shadow to maintain Darkside and deal AoE damage.

  • Employ Quietus to spend Blood on AoE damage.

  • Use Abyssal Drain for AoE damage and healing.

  • When Delirium is active, use Impalement for AoE damage.

  • Continue using Salted Earth , Salt and Darkness , and Living Shadow as in single-target rotation.

Defensive Cooldowns and Utility

  • Use The Blackest Night frequently to mitigate damage and gain free Edge of Shadow/Flood of Shadow.

  • Rotate through Rampart , Shadow Wall , and Dark Mind for sustained mitigation.

  • Employ Living Dead for situations requiring invulnerability.

  • Use Reprisal and Dark Missionary to mitigate raid-wide damage.

  • Utilize Low Blow and Interject to stun and interrupt enemy abilities.
