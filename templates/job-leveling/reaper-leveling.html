General Tips for Reaper

Maintain the Death's Design debuff on targets at all times to maximize damage output.

Unlock the Soul Gauge at level 50 to track resources for powerful abilities.

Spend Soul Reavers on Gibbet , Gallows , and Guillotine . Use Arcane Circle and Gluttony on cooldown for optimal resource management.

Unlock Enshroud through job quests for additional abilities.

Learn Soul Sow and Harvest Moon to further enhance your gameplay. Arcane Crest grants party-wide regen after shield breaks.

Enshroud interacts with Lemure's Slice and Lemure's Scythe for additional effects. Plentiful Harvest grants instant Enshroud.

Once unlocked, Communio replaces your final Enshroud GCD.

Plentiful Harvest provides Perfectio Occulta and Perfectio Parata buffs. Perfectio Parata enables casting of Perfectio, a powerful ranged attack.

Rotation Overview

Single Target

  • Levels 1-49: Maintain Shadow of Death . Use Slice > Waxing Slice > Infernal Slice .

  • Levels 50-69: Maintain Shadow of Death . Use Soul Slice on cooldown. Use Blood Stalk at 50 Soul. Continue with Slice > Waxing Slice > Infernal Slice.

  • Levels 70-79: Maintain Shadow of Death . Use Soul Slice on cooldown, Gibbet > Gallows . Use Blood Stalk or Unveiled actions as needed.

  • Level 80: Maintain Death's Design Use Gluttony , Arcane Circle , Gibbet > Gallows . Use Soul Slice, Enshroud as your priority abilities.

  • Levels 81-100: Maintain Death's Design Use Gluttony , Arcane Circle . Add Communio for AoE burst and continue with standard rotations.

Multi-Target

  • Levels 1-49: Maintain Whorl of Death . Use Spinning Scythe > Nightmare Scythe .

  • Levels 50-69: Maintain Whorl of Death . Use Grim Swathe at 50 Soul.
