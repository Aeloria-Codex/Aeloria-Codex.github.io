General Tips for Reaper

Maintain the Death's Design debuff on targets at all times.

Gain access to the Soul Gauge at level 50.

Gain access to Soul Reavers, spent on Gibbet , Gallows , or Guillotine . Use Arcane Circle and Gluttony on cooldown.

Unlock Enshroud ability through job quests.

Learn Soul Sow and Harvest Moon abilities. Arcane Crest shield break grants party a regen. Enshroud changes with Lemure's Slice and Lemure's Scythe . Plentiful Harvest enables instant Enshroud .

Once unlocked Communio replaces final Enshroud GCD.

Plentiful Harvest grants Perfectio Occulta and Perfectio Parata buffs. Perfectio Parata allows casting of Perfectio, a new powerful ranged attack.
