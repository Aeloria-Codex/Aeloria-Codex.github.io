Getting Started

Before getting started there are some things you can do speed up your leveling. Make sure to use food and Engineering Manuals.

Another Thing this guide wont cover the class quest for each job, if youre interested in that check out Crafter Class Quest

Level 1-21

At the start focus on crafting items in your crafting log. This will give bonus experience for the first time craft. Also make sure to complete your class quest for crystals so you can keep crafting.

At level 10 you can unlock Quick Synthesis, this will speed up crafting and is recomended this section.
